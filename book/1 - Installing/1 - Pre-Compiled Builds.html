Pre-compiled builds are faster and easier to use than compiling your own, and are recommended for beginners. However, they can be more than twice as slow as a self-compiled build and they may not be available for unusual hardware. If you know the command line or want extra speed you should {{1.2 - compile MCServer yourself}}.

On Linux, you can use this command to install MCServer:

curl -s https://raw.github.com/mc-server/MCServer/master/easyinstall.sh | sh

Windows builds and historical builds can be downloaded from the official builds page. For developers who want more control over the builds they download, they should visit the buildserver.
